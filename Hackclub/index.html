Denji, Chainsaw man ⛓️

Manga ~ rating ~

Denji is a poor teenage boy who lives in extreme debt after his father dies. To survive, he works as a devil hunter killing devils for money.

Denji’s only friend is a small chainsaw devil named Pochita. After Denji is betrayed and killed by the yakuza, Pochita merges with his body and becomes his heart.

Because of this fusion, Denji can transform into Chainsaw Man: Chainsaws come out of his head and arms He gains super strength and regeneration He fights powerful devils.
